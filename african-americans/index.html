Immigrant Groups

Mexicans African Americans United States Tejanos Indians Germans French Irish Polish Czech

Push Factors

African Americans were generally brought with other white immigrants as slaves.

Pull Factors

Job opportunities, the ability to escape racial hatred, and freedom from the threat of violence.

Where They Settled

African Americans were wherever their slaveholders put them to work.

Method of Travel

African American slaves were brought to Texas by boat across the Atlantic ocean from Africa.

Culture Integration

New music such as blues and rock.

Present Day Texas Presence

Approximately 11% of Texans are African American.

Evidence of Culture

Food, including ribs, cornbread, and fried okra to name a few.

Literature and poetry.

Current Events

Taste of Soul Food events, Civil Rights celebrations, Black History Month.
